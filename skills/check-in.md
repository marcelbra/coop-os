# Check-in Routine

Triggered by: "check in", "let's check in", "good morning", "morning", "start my day", "let's start"

## Steps

1. **Load context**
   - Read `backlog/docs/doc-2 - Life-Roles.md` — current state of each role
   - Read `backlog/docs/doc-3 - Recurring-Habits.md` — what Marcel is supposed to do today

2. **Check calendar**
   - Pull today's events via Google Calendar
   - Note any hard commitments, deadlines, or energy-heavy blocks

3. **Check email**
   - Scan Gmail for anything urgent or requiring action today
   - Don't summarize everything — only surface what matters

4. **Check tasks**
   - Run: `backlog task list -s "In Progress" --plain`
   - Run: `backlog task list -s "To Do" --plain`
   - Identify what's overdue, what's closest to done, what aligns with today's energy

5. **Synthesize and present**
   - Lead with: what's the one most important thing today?
   - Surface top 3 priorities (not more)
   - Flag anything urgent from email or calendar
   - Keep it tight — Marcel is checking in, not sitting through a briefing

6. **Open the floor**
   - End with: "What's on your mind?" or "Anything to add before we dive in?"
   - Be ready to shift into any skill: create_task, explore, execute_task, etc.
